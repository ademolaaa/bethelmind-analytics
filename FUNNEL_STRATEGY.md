# Bethelmind Funnel Redesign Strategy

## 1. Funnel Architecture

We are shifting from a "browse and leave" informational site to a "capture and convert" funnel.

### The Flow
1.  **Traffic Sources** (Ads, SEO, Social)
    ↓
2.  **Homepage** (The "Hub")
    *   Path A (High Intent): "Book Strategy Call"
    *   Path B (Mid Intent): "Get Free Website Audit"
    *   Path C (Specific Need): Click "Solutions" → Service Page
    ↓
3.  **Service Pages** (Specific Offer)
    *   CTA: "Get a Quote" or "Book Call"
    ↓
4.  **Qualification Step** (Filter)
    *   Simple form: Industry, Budget, Timeline.
    ↓
5.  **Booking / WhatsApp**
    *   Calendly embed or direct WhatsApp API link.
    ↓
6.  **Confirmation Page** (The "Hook")
    *   "We received your request. Here is what happens next..."
    *   "Join our WhatsApp community" (optional).
    ↓
7.  **Follow-up Sequence** (Automation)
    *   Email + WhatsApp nurturing.
    ↓
8.  **Close** (Sale).

## 4. Qualification & Automation System

### Pre-Qualifying Form Logic
*   **Budget Filter:** If budget < ₦100k, auto-reply with "Check our Starter Packages" PDF (don't book call). If > ₦200k, redirect to Calendly.
*   **Intent Filter:** If "Just researching," add to email nurture. If "ASAP," trigger "Hot Lead" alert to sales team.

### Follow-Up System (7-Day Sequence)

**Day 0 (Immediate):**
*   **WhatsApp:** "Hi [Name], thanks for booking/downloading! I'm [Your Name] from Bethelmind. I've sent the details to your email. Do you have any specific questions I can answer right here?"
*   **Email:** "Welcome! Here is your [Asset]. + One quick question."

**Day 1 (Value):**
*   **WhatsApp:** "Hey [Name], just checking in. Did you know 60% of Nigerian visitors leave sites that take >3s to load? How fast is yours?"
*   **Email:** Case Study: "How Client X got 300% more orders with this simple change."

**Day 3 (Objection Handling):**
*   **Email:** "Why custom apps beat generic templates (and save you money in the long run)."

**Day 7 (Soft Close):**
*   **WhatsApp:** "Hi [Name], are you still looking to build your [Project]? I have 2 slots left for a strategy call this week."

## 5. Measurement Plan

**Key Metrics:**
1.  **Traffic**: Unique Visitors per week.
2.  **Conversion Rate (CVR)**:
    *   Visitor → CTA Click (Target: >5%)
    *   CTA Click → Form Submit (Target: >20%)
    *   Form Submit → Showed up to Call (Target: >80%)
3.  **Cost Per Lead (CPL)**: Total Ad Spend / Qualified Leads.

**A/B Tests to Run:**
*   **Headline:** "Business Growth" (Benefit) vs. "Custom Web Development" (Feature).
*   **CTA:** "Book Strategy Call" vs. "Get a Quote".
*   **Trust:** Placing client logos *above* the fold vs. *below* the hero.
